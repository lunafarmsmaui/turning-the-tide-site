Our Solution

An Immersive Retreat That Actually Works.

Combat veterans come to Maui for 7-14 days. They work the land, surf the ocean, connect with other veterans, and reset. Always free. Built by a combat veteran, for combat veterans.

THREE PILLARS — ALL EVIDENCE-BACKED

Farm & Land Work

Agri-therapy with local Maui farmers. Plant, harvest, build. Restore daily routine and purpose.

Outdoor & Ocean

Surfing, paddling, hiking, snorkeling. Quiet the nervous system. Reconnect with the body.

Community

Small cohorts of 4-8 veterans. Peer-led. Real bonds with people who understand.

Location

Why Maui?

Our retreat is set on private farmland in Maui. The combination of warm climate, ocean access, working farmland, and the genuine warmth of the local community creates a healing environment that nowhere else on earth can match.

  • Year-round growing season — meaningful farm work in any month
  • Iconic ocean access — Ho'okipa, Kihei, Molokini, Honolua Bay
  • Haleakalā & sacred lands — natural environments that ground people fast
  • Aloha spirit — local community brings veterans into something larger than themselves
  • Far from triggering daily contexts — true reset

Evidence Base

Evidence-Backed.
Lived-Experience Built.

  • Horticultural therapy — peer-reviewed studies show reductions in PTSD symptoms, cortisol levels, and depression scores
  • Surf therapy — researched at scale by the Department of Defense for veteran PTSD; consistent positive outcomes
  • Peer support — among the strongest predictors of long-term recovery from combat PTSD
  • Nature-based interventions — proven to reduce hyperarousal and improve sleep quality
  • What's missing in conventional care: someone who's been there, an environment that's not clinical, and time

Business Model

How We Sustain It.

Turning the Tides operates as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Veteran retreats are free, funded by a diversified mix:

Foundation Grants~45%
Individual Donations~20%
Corporate Sponsorships~15%
Paid Civilian Wellness Retreats~15%
Government & Specialty Grants~5%

Civilian wellness retreats use the same land between veteran retreats — paying participants directly fund free veteran spots.
